About Us

Founding Church’s Believe:

- The church was founded in 2006-03-01 by Apostolic Bishop Jakes Mahlangu and Mrs Pastor Belina Mahlangu.

- We are a Grace Preaching Church believing in Jesus Christ as the ONLY saviour of the World.

- We are a church who believe in the Church Age or Last Day and the End of Time proceeded by the Church Rapture.

Church’s Mandate:

- Church’s primary mandate received from Jesus Christ is preaching the Grace Message in Four Coners of the Earth.

- The church has 10 Zonal Churches/Branches both in Mpumalanga and Gauteng Province as per the mandate of Christ with the intend to expand.
